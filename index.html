How-to use

Remove a piece of stikki. Make sure your hands are dry, grease-free and clean.

Step1

Loosen the kneading mass a little with your fingers. 2-3 times kneading is sufficient.

Step 2

Gently press Stikki against the object to be cleaned.

Step 3

Remove Stikki with a quick movement and free your favorite object from all dirt.

Step 4

After use, you can easily dispose of Stikki in the household waste.

FAQs

Frequently asked questions

Residue has been left on my item. What now?

Any residue can be removed by dabbing with another piece of cleaning clay. Use on absorbent surfaces may eventually leave greasy stains; these may be leave greasy stains. These can usually be removed with a dry cloth.

Is STIKKI toxic?

No. STIKKI is not toxic to humans. Nevertheless, the following applies: Caution, danger of swallowing! Keep the entire delivery out of the reach of children and animals. STIKKI can be mistaken for chewing gum. STIKKI is not suitable for consumption!

Is Stikki reusable?

For reasons of hygiene and to avoid residues, dispose of STIKKI in household waste after single use.
